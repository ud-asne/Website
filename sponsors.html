// Marine Technology Partner
Seafloor Systems
Autonav Controller and RC Unit - Full sponsor

A leading manufacturer of Unmanned Surface Vessels and hydrographic survey technology with 25+ years in the marine industry — a perfect technical partner for our autonomous marine program.

// Propulsion Technology Partner

Brushless Motors — Sponsored Discount

Supplying our primary drive motors and backup units. Flipsky's brushless underwater motor platform forms the heart of our 13,000 RPM propulsion system running at 53V.

// Components Partner

Components & Systems — Sponsored Discounts

Supporting the team with component discounts across multiple systems, enabling us to source high-quality hardware within our student budget.
